When symptoms are dismissed.

Systemic gender bias in healthcare contributes to delayed diagnoses, undertreatment, and avoidable harm for women. This project collects evidence, data visualisations and real patient stories to raise awareness and drive change.

See statistics Read case studies

Quick facts

Higher misdiagnosis
Women experience higher rates of misdiagnosis for several conditions (see sources).
Longer diagnosis delays
Average time-to-diagnosis is often longer for women than men for certain chronic conditions.
Pain undertreatment
Studies show women are less likely to receive adequate pain management.
